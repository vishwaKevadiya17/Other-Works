Science helps me understand how the world works. I enjoy learning about atoms, planets, and the human body. Water is H2O, and Einstein's equation is E = mc2.

Mathematics teaches me how to solve problems. I like algebra, geometry, and statistics. I also like writing equations like a2 + b2 = c2.

I love learning how computers work and how to code. My favorite coding languages are:

  1. HTML — for building web pages
