🌟 The Tale of the Lost Star

Once upon a time, in a small town, there lived a young girl named Lily. One night, Lily saw a bright little star fall from the sky and disappear into the forest.

Curious and excited, Lily packed her bag and walked into the forest. She met talking birds, friendly rabbits, and a wise old owl who helped her find the shining star.

The little star was sad because it had lost its way home. Lily promised to help. Together they climbed the tallest hill where the moon showed them the path back to the sky.

The star thanked Lily by filling the night with sparkling light. From that day on, whenever Lily looked at the stars, she remembered her magical adventure.

✨ Moral: Kindness and courage always light the way.
